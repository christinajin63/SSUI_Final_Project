The student-run Lunar Gala began in 1997 as a celebration of the Chinese New Year; subsequent years have been thematically based on the zodiac. With 2018 being the Year of the Dog, Grace Kao, Gilly Johnson and Jarret Lin, producers of this year's Gala, chose "Ferox" as the show's title and guiding principle.
